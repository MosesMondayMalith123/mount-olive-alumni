About the Class of 2017

The Class of 2017 at Mount Olive Good Shepherd Primary School was one of the most historic and unforgettable classes in the school’s history. This was the year candidates officially used the UNEB examination center number, marking a major academic milestone.

The year was filled with intense academic competition, discipline, teamwork, and strong friendship bonds. Students actively participated in exams, sports, Music Dance and Drama (MDD), debates, and class activities.

A unique feature of this class was the Manila board leadership system inside the classroom. Moses led Mathematics while Opiyo led Science, both performing at the top from January to November.

It also marked the emotional farewell of great teachers: Tr. Paul, Tr. Anthony, Tr. Charles, and Tr. Robert.
